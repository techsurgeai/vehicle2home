Smart Charging Reduces Fleet Energy Costs

A regional delivery fleet operating in a high-demand urban area struggled with rising electricity costs after transitioning to electric vehicles. Initially, vehicles were charged immediately after returning to the depot, often during peak tariff hours. To address this, the company implemented a smart charging system that scheduled charging during off-peak periods when electricity rates were significantly lower.
